How to get your CAS Statement

  1. Go to CAMS CAS Statement
  2. Select Statement Type: Summary (Only balances and valuations)
  3. Set "As On" to today's date
  4. Folio Listing: Without zero balance folios
  5. Enter your registered email and PAN
  6. Set a password and click Submit
  7. Check your email for the PDF attachment
